### So, how do we outcompete them here?

To win with content creation, there are ultimately 3 levers:

1. Vastness - how much content do we have vs competitors?
2. Momentum - how frequently are we posting new content and updating existing? And is it increasing?
3. Depth - how information dense, unique and valuable is our content?

We aim to win in all 3.

### How much content is needed? (Vastness)

| **Competitor** | **Traffic** | **Content** | **Products** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **lazzoni.com** | 82,671 | 297 | 1,701 |
| **italian-interiors.com** | 10,547 | 546 | 9,722 |
| **michelangelodesigns.com** | 5,741 | 269 | 3,003 |

Competitors that have a lot of content, have more trust with Google and get more traffic in return.

For reference, D&D has currently 6 content pieces published.

Looking at competitors, we can see that 250+ content pieces is definitely needed to compete.

### How many should we publish a month? (Momentum)

Competitors have slown down now, but we need to look at where they started:

| **lazzoni** | Launched Q4 2021 (100 pages), steady ~15/q since |  |  | **100** | 65 | 36 | 36 | 60 |
| **italian-interiors** | Big push Q1 2021 (222 pgs), sporadic since, 80% stale | 46 |  | **265** | 25 | 114 | 48 | 10 |
| **michelangelodesigns** | Big push Q3 2019 (110 pgs), steady ~5/q + heavy updates | **111** | 17 | 22 | 22 | 31 | 20 | 16 |

All the winners here started off with a bang and kept a steady pace. But we need to catch up to them THIS year, so we’ll publish a big batch, and continue publishing with high momentum.

### How frequently should we update the content? (Momentum)

| **Competitor** | **Traffic** | **Pages** | **Updates/yr** | **Update Cycle** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **lazzoni.com** | 82,671 | 297 | 706 | Every ~5 months |
| **michelangelodesigns.com** | 5,741 | 269 | 474 | Every ~7 months |
| **calligarisnyc.com** | 1,906 | 41 | 56 | Every ~9 months |
| **italian-interiors.com** | 10,547 | 546 | 266 | Every ~2 years |

We can beat this by updating every 2-3 months. We wanna keep these frequent - each content update gives Google a signal to re-evaluate the page, and if it’s better → rank it higher.

### How do we make better content than the competitors? (Depth)

1. Each content piece is constructed out of competitive analysis and research, to exceed competitors in depth.
2. Content writing is grounded in style guides and the creative brief to be on-brand and stand out against the competing content.
3. The knowledge base is constructed and used to create value-dense content at scale
    1. keeps information consistent, and powers the content updating/improvement cycle too.

### SEO business value calculation formula

Data we need:

- T - Total potential traffic
- E - A pessimistic traffic estimate which we could get from the total potential traffic
- P - Average deal size

Calculation:

If one client is worth ~x and y% or amount of visitors buy from the amount of traffic we get them, it would equal to this much money

T x {0.? - pessimistic percentage of how much traffic we can get} = E

E x {0.? - industry avg. conversion rate} = C a.k.a how many people from the estimated traffic buy
